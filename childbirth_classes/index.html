Black Sheep Birth Services offers in-person, Home & Birth Center Childbirth Classes.

If you are planning to have your baby at home or at a freestanding birth center, this is the class for you! Recommended by local midwives. Come learn about the process of labor and birth, comfort measure techniques, relaxation methods, interventions, & more. Classes are typically held every other month and taught at Willow Women's & Childrens Wellness (aka the Aurora Birth Center). This class is divided into five, 2.5 hour sessions and is taught by a Certified Childbirth Educator (CBE). Each session is scheduled to begin promptly at 6:00 PM.* The investment is $250 with a $75.00 nonrefundable deposit due at the time of registration to guarantee your spot.

Important:

  • We recommend that you schedule your class to begin around 30-34 weeks gestation so you have time to complete the series before you give birth. (Remember, babies don't always come on their due date and you'll want to be prepared!)
  • This class is designed for couples because we want both partners to be well prepared. (If you are a single parent or have another unique situation, please don't hesitate to contact us to discuss.)
  • This class is open to the public. Clients of the Aurora Birth Center receive a $25 discount on their tuition. Space is limited.
  • These classes will be held at the Willow Women's & Children's Wellness (the Aurora Birth Center), 21358 HWY 99E, Aurora, Oregon 97002
  • *Dates are subject to change if the need arises. Please confirm class dates & times before completing your registration.

About Birth Boot Camp

Birth Boot Camp specializes in preparing couples for an amazing birth and breastfeeding experience. Through comprehensive online childbirth classes, incredible in-person childbirth classes, and our quickly growing doula program, we are able to reach couples around the world.

A Class to Meet Your Needs

We offer the most robust options for childbirth education.

Whether you're giving birth to your first or fourth baby, planning a home or hospital birth, or just need some nutritional guidance for pregnancy and beyond, we've got a class for that! Topics range from comfort measures for labor to breastfeeding and newborn care.

Online Childbirth Education

 Unable to attend a live class?

In person classes are great for building relationships and meeting like-minded couples, but our lives are busy and calendars don't always align. Some partners work away from home or have varying schedules. We totally get it! You can still have an amazing birth by taking a Birth Boot Camp Class online!

Birth Boot Camp DOULAS

Amazing Support for Birth

When planning for an amazing birth, education is key! However, when the classes are over and you still need support at the birth itself, hiring a Birth Boot Camp Doula can help. Studies have shown that having a doula decreases the risk of having a cesarean, shortens the length of labor, and improves the overall satisfaction of the birth experience.
